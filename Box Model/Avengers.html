Avengers

The Avengers are a team of superheroes from Marvel Comics who unite to protect the world from threats too great for any single hero. The original lineup included Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye, each bringing unique powers and personalities. Over time, the team expanded to include heroes like Scarlet Witch, Vision, Black Panther, and Spider-Man. Their stories highlight courage, teamwork, and sacrifice as they face powerful villains such as Loki and Thanos, making the Avengers a symbol of hope and resilience in the Marvel Universe.
